Circuitary Workshops

Bluetooth Car Project

A Bluetooth car project involves building a remote-controlled car that is controlled using a Bluetooth connection between the car and a mobile device or computer. The project typically involves selecting a hardware platform, assembling the car, programming the microcontroller, and establishing a Bluetooth connection.

Monster Design

Circuitry workshop conducted by Robotics Club MMMUT is a hands-on training program that focuses on teaching participants the fundamental concepts and skills related to electronics and circuit design. During the workshop, participants will learn how to work with electronic components, read and design circuit diagrams, and build their own circuits.
